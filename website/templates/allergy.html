Red Onion

Properties: Red onion contains all the vitamins from the B group, vitamin C, as well as several important minerals for the body: phosphorus, sodium, calcium, magnesium, zinc, iron, and chromium.

Green Apples

Properties: Green apples are rich in fiber and can help reduce cholesterol levels in the blood. They also contain antioxidant substances that help prevent skin cancer.

Allergens: pollen, proteins

Pears

Properties: Pears are extremely healthy, having a high content of fiber, vitamins, as well as valuable antioxidants that protect the body and strengthen the immune system.

Allergens: pollen, proteins

Blackberries

Properties: One serving of blackberries provides approximately half of the recommended daily intake of antioxidant vitamin C. Blackberries are also a source of vitamin K and offer modest amounts of potassium, magnesium, vitamin A, iron, and calcium.

Allergens: fructose, glucose, carbohydrates, sucrose

Smoked Cheese

Properties: Smoked cheese is a highly nutritious and biologically valuable food due to its content of proteins, lipids, vitamins, and minerals.

Allergens: lactose, proteins, lipids
